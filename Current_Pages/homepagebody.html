Introducing the STEAM Center!
The Allen ISD STEAM Center is a one-of-a-kind facility dedicated to science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics. The STEAM Center offers a foundational learning system for students of all ages, fostering interest, developing future-ready talents, and training Allen ISD students for success in STEAM related fields..
What the STEAM Center Has to Offer
The STEAM Center has purposefully designed spaces that provide an innovative learning environment both inside the building and outdoors. In addition to the 111,000 square-foot facility, the STEAM Center property includes a pond, nature trails and outdoor learning spaces to be used by all grade levels.
